Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you create an account, along with the expense records you log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the core features of Spendly: recording expenses, generating spending summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely in our database. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting and email delivery) solely to operate the service, and these providers are bound to handle data only as instructed.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
